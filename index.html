Built by Medical Students - Our Story

PDF2Anki started as a simple idea: to make flashcard creation easier and more accessible for students around the world.

From lecture slides to notes, we wanted to harness the power of AI to automate the flashcard creation process, freeing up valuable study time.

Our journey began in our medical common room, fueled by late-night coding sessions and a passion for education. Today, PDF2Anki has grown into a tool used by thousands of students across the globe.

We're proud of what we've achieved, and we're committed to continuing our mission of making study tools smarter and more efficient.

  • Is PDF2Anki free?

    Yes, PDF2Anki offers a free community tier that allows users to generate flashcards from their PDFs. There is also a Power User plan available with additional features and increased limits.

  • What is the maximum file size for PDF uploads?

    The free community tier has a file size limit of 5 MB and 5000 words. Power Users have a higher limit of 200 MB and 50,000 words. You can upgrade to a Power User plan to enjoy higher limits.

  • Can I edit or delete flashcards after they are generated?

    Yes, PDF2Anki provides an intuitive interface that allows you to edit or delete individual flashcards before downloading them.

  • How does the AI model generate flashcards?

    PDF2Anki uses advanced AI models to analyze the text in your PDF and generate flashcards in the form of questions and answers. The AI is designed to identify key concepts and present them in a flashcard format to foster active recall.

  • Can I print the generated flashcards?

    Yes, PDF2Anki offers an option to download the generated flashcards as a printable PDF, allowing you to print and use them as physical flashcards.

  • Can I generate flashcards in multiple languages?

    PDF2Anki supports flashcard generation in various languages. You can select your preferred language when creating flashcards. If your language is not there, feel free to email us at pdf2anki@gmail.com and we will add it soon!

  • How can I subscribe/unscribe to a Power User plan?

    You can upgrade to a Power User plan by pressing Power User on the side bar. Power Users enjoy additional features, increased limits, and priority support. You can also unsubscribe anytime by pressing "Manage Account" on the sidebar.

  • What if I encounter an issue or need help?

    We are here to assist you! If you encounter any issues or have any questions, please contact our support team at pdf2anki@gmail.com.
